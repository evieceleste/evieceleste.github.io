Bio

Evie Celeste is a Choreographer and Director who uses her versatility in the dance and musical theatre sector to become an asset to any project. She has a multitude of credits as a Choreographer, Director, Performer and Professional Dancer working on a global scale.

Evie has choreographed and directed for numerous production shows on varying contracts worldwide and at sea.

Evie choreographs and supplies dancers to work at corporate events, weddings and additionally runs masterclasses and workshops for a variety of ages.
