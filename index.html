About me,
Evie Celeste

I am often described as an artist, my belief is that this has something to do with my quirky personality, my energetic zest for life and my determination to succeed.

From being a tap dancing penguin at the age of 5 to being a glamorous champignon on the Disney parades. I have never forgotten how fortunate I am to be doing something that I love. This is why I train hard, work hard, and always remain professional.

I am passionate about creating new work, love embarking on new adventures and building new friendships. I find it incredibly rewarding taking a leadership role within a creative environment and additionally enjoy being an integral cast member.

If you require any more information about me don't hesitate to get in touch.
